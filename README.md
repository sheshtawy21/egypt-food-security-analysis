# 🇪🇬 Egypt Food Security & Economic Affordability Analysis (2020–2024)
### *Unmasking the Illusion of Wealth Behind Egypt's Rising Wages*

> 💥 Between 2020 and 2024, Egypt's minimum wage **tripled** — yet minimum-wage households could afford **less food than ever before**. This project uncovers the hidden collision between currency collapse and food inflation, revealing the true cost of survival behind Egypt's headline economic numbers.

---

## 💡 The Illusion of Wealth & Economic Reality

A rising paycheck *feels* like progress. But when currency devaluation and inflation outpace income growth, that progress becomes a **statistical illusion** — one that conceals a very real erosion of purchasing power.

Between 2020 and 2024, Egypt was hit by a rare and brutal **compounding shock**: a sharp devaluation of the Egyptian Pound struck at the exact same time as an explosive surge in food prices — both landing squarely on minimum-wage households.

This project looks past nominal income to measure what actually matters: **how much food, stability, and security a household's earnings can genuinely buy.** The result is a data-driven picture of the widening gap between *looking* wealthier on paper and *being* wealthier in reality.

---

## 📊 Key Project Highlights

### 🔺 The Double Shock
A currency crisis and a food-price crisis, colliding at the same moment:
- 💱 **59.84%** devaluation of the Egyptian Pound
- 🍗 **63.57%** food inflation rate
- ⚡ Together: a **compounded affordability crisis** for minimum-wage households

### 🎭 The Illusion of Wealth
Wages tripled on paper — real buying power told a very different story:
- 💰 Nominal minimum wage: **EGP 2,000** (2020) → **EGP 6,000** (2024) — a full **3× increase**
- 📦 Real purchasing power ("Units Affordable"): **303 units → 258 units** — a clear **decline**
- 🧮 Verdict: a bigger paycheck, but a smaller basket

### 🐔 The Smoking Gun
One statistic that captures the entire crisis in a single number:
- In **2023**, a single unit of **Farm Chicken** consumed **76.08%** of a minimum-wage earner's **entire monthly income**
- One survival staple. Almost an entire paycheck.

### 🌍 Macro Trade Reality
Global markets got cheaper — Egypt's import bill didn't:
- 📉 Global food commodity costs actually **fell** to **$0.98/ton**
- 📈 Egypt's local import bill **exploded**: **EGP 16.2 Billion → EGP 119.2 Billion**
- 🔍 The cause wasn't world prices — it was **currency collapse**, magnifying the same import volume into a far larger EGP bill
